Guillermo Salazar, Landscape Designer & Consultant provides his knowledge and expertise through personalized consultation to Individuals, Homeowners, Associations, and Commercial properties. 

Mr. Salazar expertise includes two years of Landscape Architecture from University of Florida and a bachelor’s degree in Landscape design and Management from Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University as well as more than 15 years of experience in the grow, care and maintenance of tropical and subtropical species of Florida and the tropical Americas. Growing as a child of two landscape designers in Central America he had the opportunity to grow as a natural designer, and to enrich his knowledge even before his college years. Guillermo has been recognized with different awards since then and throughout his years as a professional Landscape designer in Florida and internationally. His specialty ranges from professional landscape design plans, renovation or replanting of existing landscape beds to interior decorating with live plants, unique pool pot design, as well as commercial or new projects. His strengths rely on his vast knowledge of design techniques, palms, and tropical plants of Florida to create the perfect color palette and texture that would transform your yard in your own private paradise garden.
