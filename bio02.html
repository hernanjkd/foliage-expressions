Guillermo Salazar, landscape designer and consultant, provides his knowledge and expertise through personalized consultation to individuals, homeowners, associations and commercial properties.

Mr. Salazar's expertise includes:
- Two years of Landscape Architecture from the University of Florida.
- A bachelor's degree in landscape design and management from Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University.
- A master's degree in horticultural science from University of Florida.
- ISA certified Arborist.
- More than 20 years of experience in the grow, care and maintenance of tropical and subtropical plant species of Florida and the tropical Americas.

Growing as a child of two landscape designers in Central America, Guillermo had the opportunity to grow as a natural designer and to enrich his knowledge even before his college years. Guillermo has been recognized with different awards since then and throughout his years as a professional landscape designer in Florida and internationally.

Guillermo's specialty ranges from professional landscape design plans, renovation or replanting of existing landscape beds to interior decorating with live plants, orchid maintenance and care, unique pool pot design, as well as commercial or new projects. His strengths rely on his vast knowledge of design techniques, palms and tropical plants of Florida to create the perfect color palette and texture that would transform your yard into your own private paradise garden.
