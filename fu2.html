Water features add the sense of sound to the garden.  Water also creates calming and soothing feelings.  Visually water can be the focal point of a garden design or it can be a small detail with only a trickling sound.  Each water feature that we design is a one of a kind creation. A water feature may be designed to compliment the clients’ style as well as its surroundings, whether the style of the client or the architecture is contemporary or naturalistic.
